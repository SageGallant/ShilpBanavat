Inspired by traditional Rajasthani pottery techniques dating back centuries, this handcrafted ceramic vase embodies the perfect blend of tradition and contemporary aesthetics. Each piece is individually thrown on a potter's wheel before being hand-painted with intricate designs using natural dyes and pigments.

The skilled artisans apply multiple layers of glaze and fire the pieces at high temperatures to achieve the unique, rich finish that makes each vase one-of-a-kind. The slightly irregular textures and subtle variations in color are hallmarks of authentic handcrafted ceramics, making each piece truly special.

These vases not only serve as beautiful decorative elements but also tell a story of cultural heritage and artistic excellence. Perfect for displaying fresh flowers or simply as a standalone statement piece, this vase will bring warmth and character to any space.

Origin

Handcrafted in Jaipur, Rajasthan by skilled artisans with generations of experience in traditional pottery making.

Master Potter Ramesh Ji

Jaipur, Rajasthan

Ramesh Ji comes from a family that has practiced pottery for seven generations in the outskirts of Jaipur. Trained by his father since the age of ten, he has mastered the traditional techniques of Rajasthani pottery while incorporating contemporary design elements that appeal to modern aesthetics.

His workshop employs fifteen artisans from his village, creating sustainable livelihoods and preserving cultural techniques that might otherwise be lost. Each piece that comes out of his studio carries the distinct mark of excellence and attention to detail that Ramesh Ji is known for.

"Clay speaks to me," says Ramesh Ji. "When I sit at my wheel, my hands know the language of the earth, and together we create something that carries both tradition and a piece of my soul."

Ramesh Ji has received national recognition for his contribution to Indian crafts, including the prestigious Shilp Guru Award in 2018.
